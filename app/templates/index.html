What is it about?

This event is about the sustainable creation of useful software in a responsible way.

People

Everyone who is concerned with coding, testing, code quality, software craftsmanship…

  • You are passionate about software development and testing.
  • You have already heard of Software Craftsmanship or even already are part of the raising community.
  • You want to share your knowlege, experience and expertise.

Action

The event will be run as a non-profit, low-cost event. It is a joint-effort of all Softwerkskammer groups, the community of all German Software Craftsmanship groups. It will be much like a retreat. We will be there for 48 hours to collaborate and share ideas. There will be:

  • Highly collaborative interactions
  • Lightning talks
  • Self-organized Open Space
